Our Story of Excellence

For over three decades, we've been creating unforgettable experiences in the heart of the city

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a symbol of luxury

1990

The Beginning

Grand Horizon Hotel opened its doors with just 50 rooms, founded by visionary entrepreneur Alemayehu Bekele.

2000

First Expansion

Added the East Wing with 100 luxury suites and our first fine dining restaurant.

2010

Five-Star Recognition

Awarded our first five-star rating and recognized as Ethiopia's top luxury hotel.

2020

Modern Renovation

Completed a comprehensive renovation adding eco-friendly features and smart room technology.

Present

Continuing Excellence

Serving over 500,000 guests annually with 300+ dedicated staff members.
